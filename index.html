Step 2 of 2 — Tax profile

EOPT classification (RR 8-2024). For Micro/Small individuals, also surfaces the optional 1701-MS annual ITR.
OSD or 8% on a pure SE filer routes to 1701A; Itemized routes to 1701.

Employer SSS · PhilHealth · Pag-IBIG remittances are automatically tracked when Has employees is enabled above.

Rate reference (2025 — verify for updates after Aug 2025)
SSS (RA 11199, CI-2024-006 to CI-2024-010, effective Jan 2025)
  Rate: 15% of MSC · ER 10% + EE 5%
  MSC range: ₱5,000–₱35,000
  EC Fund: ₱10/mo (MSC < ₱15,000) or ₱30/mo (MSC ≥ ₱15,000) — employer only
  MySSS Pension Booster / MPF: +1% of excess MSC above ₱20,000 (ER + EE shared)
  Voluntary: member pays full 15% of chosen MSC
  Deadline: last business day of the month following the applicable month (staggered by employer SSS number)

PhilHealth (RA 11223, 2025 rate)
  Rate: 5% of basic monthly salary · ER 2.5% + EE 2.5%
  Floor: ₱10,000/mo (₱500 total · ₱250 per side)
  Ceiling: ₱100,000/mo (₱5,000 total · ₱2,500 per side)
  Voluntary: member pays full 5% of declared monthly income
  Deadline: last day of the month following the applicable month

Pag-IBIG / HDMF (RA 9679)
  Rate: ER 2% + member 2% (compensation > ₱1,500); ER 2% + member 1% (≤ ₱1,500)
  Contribution ceiling: ₱10,000/mo → max ₱200 each side (HDMF Circular 460, eff. Feb 2024)
  Voluntary: member may pay above the mandatory minimum
  Deadline: 10th of the month following the applicable month
