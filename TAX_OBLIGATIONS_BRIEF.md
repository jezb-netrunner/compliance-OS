# Philippine Tax Obligations Brief

> Extracted from The Present Value — BIR Compliance Calendar & Calculator  
> Jurisdiction: Philippines | Authority: Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR)  
> Governing law: NIRC (RA 8424) as amended by TRAIN (RA 10963), CREATE (RA 11534), EOPT (RA 11976), CREATE MORE (RA 12066), and related revenue regulations.  
> Reviewed as of: 23 April 2026.

> ⚠ **Time-sensitive override (CY 2025 ITR):** Per **RMC 30-2026** (pursuant to EO 110, s. 2026), the filing deadline for **2025 Annual Income Tax Returns** (1700, 1701, 1701A, 1702-RT/MX/EX), payment of the corresponding taxes, and submission of required attachments is moved from **15 April 2026 → 15 May 2026**. Quarterly VAT/percentage tax deadlines are **not** extended.

---

## 1. Tax Regimes (Self-Employed / Mixed Income)

| Regime | Rate | Who qualifies |
|---|---|---|
| 8% Flat Income Tax | 8% of gross sales/receipts (purely SE: less the ₱250,000 exemption; mixed income: no ₱250K deduction) | Gross sales ≤ ₱3M VAT threshold; in lieu of graduated income tax + percentage tax under Sec. 116; elected on the first 1701Q of the year or on registration |
| Graduated Income Tax | 0–35% per bracket | Default; applies to all income levels |
| Optional Standard Deduction (OSD) | **Individuals:** 40% of gross sales/receipts; **Corporations:** 40% of gross income | Elected in lieu of itemized deductions; irrevocable for the taxable year (NIRC Sec. 34(L), RR 16-2008) |
| Itemized Deductions | Actual allowable expenses | Must be substantiated with receipts/records |

### Graduated Income Tax Brackets (TRAIN Law)

| Annual Taxable Income | Rate |
|---|---|
| Up to ₱250,000 | 0% |
| ₱250,001 – ₱400,000 | 15% on excess over ₱250,000 |
| ₱400,001 – ₱800,000 | ₱22,500 + 20% on excess over ₱400,000 |
| ₱800,001 – ₱2,000,000 | ₱102,500 + 25% on excess over ₱800,000 |
| ₱2,000,001 – ₱8,000,000 | ₱402,500 + 30% on excess over ₱2,000,000 |
| Over ₱8,000,000 | ₱2,202,500 + 35% on excess over ₱8,000,000 |

## 2. Corporate Income Tax

| Tax | Rate | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Regular Corporate Income Tax (RCIT) | 25% standard; **20% for small domestic corps** | Small-corp 20% applies to domestic corps with net taxable income ≤ ₱5M **and** total assets ≤ ₱100M (excluding land). CREATE (RA 11534). |
| RCIT — RBEs under Enhanced Deduction Regime (EDR) | **20%** | CREATE MORE (RA 12066, effective 28 Nov 2024); applies only to Registered Business Enterprises electing EDR. Non-RBE corps keep the 25% / small-corp 20% rule above. |
| Minimum Corporate Income Tax (MCIT) | 2% of gross income | Applies from the 4th year of operations; whichever is higher vs. RCIT. Historical: **1% from 1 Jul 2020 to 30 Jun 2023** under CREATE; reverted to 2% from 1 Jul 2023 (RMC 69-2023, RMC 36-2024). |

---

## 3. Business Taxes

| Tax | Rate | Threshold / Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Value Added Tax (VAT) | 12% on sales/services | Mandatory registration once gross sales exceed ₱3M/year. Filed **quarterly only** via 2550Q under EOPT (RA 11976); BIR Form 2550M is abolished. |
| Percentage Tax | 3% of gross sales/receipts | For non-VAT-registered taxpayers not using the 8% flat rate. Historical: **1% from 1 Jul 2020 to 30 Jun 2023** under CREATE; reverted to 3% from 1 Jul 2023 (RMC 69-2023). |
| Government VAT Withholding | 5% of gross payment | **Creditable** (not final) withholding under TRAIN, effective 1 Jan 2021. Withholding agent issues **BIR Form 2306**; supplier credits the 5% against output VAT on the 2550Q (NIRC Sec. 114(C)). |

---

## 4. Withholding Taxes

| Tax | Rate | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Expanded Withholding Tax (EWT) | 1–15% (varies by transaction; see RR 11-2018 as amended) | Withheld by payor; creditable against payee's income tax. **Deductibility note:** Under EOPT (RA 11976) the former Sec. 34(K) of the NIRC was repealed — an expense is deductible even if the related tax was not withheld (the withholding-agent penalties under Secs. 247–251 still apply separately). |
| Final Withholding Tax (FWT) | Varies | On dividends, royalties, interest, and certain other passive income; not creditable. |
| Creditable Withholding Tax on Compensation | Per graduated bracket (RR 11-2018 Annex E, updated by RR 29-2025 for de minimis caps) | Withheld from employee salaries per payroll period. |

## 5. Other Taxes & Payroll Caps

| Item | Notes |
|---|---|
| Documentary Stamp Tax (DST) | On taxable documents: leases, loans, debt instruments, certain stock transactions (NIRC Title VII). |

| Monetized value of vacation and sick leave (government) | Full amount |
| Medical cash allowance to dependents | ₱2,000 per semester (₱333/month) |
| Rice subsidy | ₱2,500/month or one 50-kg sack |
| Uniform / clothing allowance | ₱8,000/year |
| Actual medical assistance to employee | ₱12,000/year |
| Laundry allowance | ₱400/month |
| Employee achievement awards (tangible property) | ₱12,000/year |
| Christmas / major-anniversary gifts | ₱6,000 per employee/year |
| Daily meal allowance (overtime / graveyard) | 25% of basic minimum wage |
| CBA and productivity incentives (combined) | ₱10,000/year |

## 9. Key Thresholds & Rules

- **VAT registration threshold:** ₱3,000,000 gross annual sales/receipts (NIRC Sec. 109(CC)).
- **8% flat rate eligibility:** Gross sales ≤ ₱3,000,000; purely self-employed may apply 8% on sales in **excess of ₱250,000**; mixed-income earners apply 8% on the full business gross (no ₱250K deduction).
- **OSD base:** Individuals — 40% of gross sales/receipts; Corporations — 40% of gross income.
- **MCIT:** 2% of gross income from the 4th year of corporate operations (1% during 1 Jul 2020 – 30 Jun 2023 under CREATE).
- **CREATE MORE (RA 12066, eff. 28 Nov 2024):** 20% RCIT for RBEs under the Enhanced Deduction Regime; shifts VAT remittance on RBE local sales to the buyer; 90-day Sec. 112 input-VAT refund window for claims filed from 1 Apr 2025.
- **Monthly 2550M abolished:** Under EOPT (RA 11976, RR 3-2024); VAT is filed quarterly only via 2550Q.
- **Annual Registration Fee (₱500, Form 0605) abolished:** Under EOPT (RA 11976 Sec. 21, RR 7-2024), effective 22 January 2024.
- **Sec. 34(K) repealed (EOPT):** Expense deductibility no longer conditioned on prior withholding; withholding-agent penalties under Secs. 247–251 still apply separately.
- **EWT sworn declaration:** Filed by January 15 each year by self-employed/mixed income earners qualifying for a lower withholding rate.
- **13th month pay:** Statutory release by **December 24** (P.D. 851); aggregate "13th month + other benefits" tax-exempt up to **₱90,000** (TRAIN).
- **De minimis benefit ceilings:** Updated by **RR 29-2025**, effective 6 January 2026 — see Section 5.
- **E-invoicing:** **RR 11-2025 as amended by RR 26-2025** — 31 December 2026 deadline for covered taxpayers (Large Taxpayers, e-commerce, POS/CAS/CBA users); **RR 1-2026** — same 31 December 2026 deadline for RBEs to update invoicing systems to tag "VAT on Local Sales."
- **CY 2025 Annual ITR deadline:** Extended from 15 April 2026 to **15 May 2026** per RMC 30-2026 (EO 110, s. 2026).
